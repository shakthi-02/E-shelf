Book Genres

There are various book genres to choose from.

Biography

A biography, or simply bio, is a detailed description of a person's life. It involves more than just basic facts like education, work, relationships, and death; it portrays a person's experience of these life events.

History

History books refer to the record of past events.

Science Fiction

Science fiction genre deals with imaginative and futuristic concepts.

Poetry

Poetry is a form of literary art that uses aesthetic and rhythmic qualities of language to evoke meanings.

Fantasy

Fantasy is a genre which involves themes of the supernatural, magic, and imaginary worlds and creatures.

Mystery

Mystery is a fiction genre where the nature of an event remains mysterious until the end of the story.
